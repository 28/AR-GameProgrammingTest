1. NPCs
An NPC has several attributes: name, age, hair color, job, family members.
There are several types of jobs, such as farmer, logger, fisherman, scribe, armor merchant, weapon merchant,
magic merchant, guard, innkeeper. An NPC can only have one job at a time but can change jobs over time. Each job has
unique functionality performed by the NPC when working. An NPC has N+ family members and they can be of different types,
like: brother, sister, mother, father, son, daughter

Design a class structure to meet the above requirements.
